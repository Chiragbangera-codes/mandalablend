# Design System Walkthrough & Verification

All 11 Chapters of the Mandala Blend design specification have been fully implemented and verified! The website now functions as a high-end, premium digital art gallery with seamless visual rhythm and fully verified user flows.

---

## 🎨 Implementation Highlights

### 1. Foundation & Typography (Chapters 1 & 9)
- **Primary Color Palette**: Entire site set to a neutral `#FAFAFA` gallery background, keeping the focus entirely on the artwork.
- **Accent**: All primary CTA highlights, links, and active elements updated to deep Wine `#7A1738`.
- **Typography Scale**: Enforced Playfair Display for headings and Poppins for clean body copy. Hero title stands at 72px desktop/42px mobile.
- **Spacing**: Strict implementation of the 8-point grid spacing system across all modules.

### 2. Global Components (Chapter 2)
- **Announcement Bar**: Light grey `#F8F8F8` top bar displaying free shipping info in Poppins 13px.
- **Navbar**: Centered navigation menu with sticky offset, hover underline, and Collection categories dropdown. Included social media navigation icons.
- **Footer**: Refactored into a dark `#1F1F22` 5-column layout containing a brand bio, newsletter sign-up, customer support links, and copyright text.
- **Floating WhatsApp**: Positioned bottom-right with an idle 6s vertical hover-float animation.

### 3. Home Page (Chapter 3)
- **Redesigned Hero**: 45/55 grid layout with a dramatic "Mandala Art made with *love*" Playfair heading.
- **Why Choose Us**: Light grey background containing 4 brand columns separated by thin vertical dividers.
- **Instagram Gallery**: Clean horizontal strip displaying 7 posts with hover zoom and 15% opacity overlays.

### 4. Collections Page (Chapter 4)
- **Filters & Toolbar**: Sticky filter panel with price range slider and availability selectors. Integrated product results counter.
- **Pagination**: Fully responsive buttons with wine accent status indications.

### 5. Product Details (Chapter 5)
- **Interactive Gallery**: Dual arrow controls, responsive thumbnail selectors, and custom tabs (Description, Materials, Care, Shipping).
- **CTA Link**: Generates customized WhatsApp messages containing product name, selected size, and quantity.

### 6. Gallery Page (Chapter 6)
- **Featured Custom Orders**: Highlighting unique custom works with dual columns displaying customer stories, inspirations, completion time, and materials.
- **Statistics Counter**: Animated number ticks rising on scroll (e.g. 1500+ Completed Orders).
- **Lightbox**: Implemented keyboard arrow navigation, swipe events, and escape-key listener.

### 7. About & Contact Pages (Chapters 7 & 8)
- **About Page**: Alternating Brand Story rows, Meet the Artist section with Jaipur background, and a "From Idea to Artwork" workflow timeline.
- **Contact Page**: 2x2 grid contact cards, WhatsApp submissions form, and smooth FAQ accordions.
